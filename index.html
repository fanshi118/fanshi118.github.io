About me

Hello there.

Welcome to my site. I'm currently studying Data Science at New York University, expected to graduate May 2017. Originally from Beijing, China, I was an Electrical Engineering undergrad at the University of Southern California before moving to New York City in 2015 for my master's degree.

Currently, I'm a Data Science Intern at Space Jam Data, a real estate tech startup, learning to apply natural language processing techniques to extract consumer insights. Meanwhile, I worked at NYU Data Services as a student consultant, helping NYU students resolve technical issues they face while using GIS and analytics software.

Previously, I had some experience with computer vision, a neat area that combines my EE background with DS. I used to work with ARGO Labs on their street quality image project, supervised by Varun Adibhatla. My work at ARGO culminated in a technical paper presented at Bloomberg's Data for Good Exchange 2016, a nationwide civic data scinece conference.

Enjoying reading data journalism articles on a daily basis, I'm trying to gain skills in interactive data visualization. In the near future, I hope to be able to develop data science products to address the challenges in various industries, preferably in the context of text analytics.
