About me

Hello there.

Welcome to my site. I graduated from New York University's Data Science program in May 2017. Originally from Beijing, China, I studied Electrical Engineering as an undergrad at the University of Southern California before moving to New York City in 2015 for my master's degree.

I'm a Data Scientist at L2, spending my time crunching numbers on brands. Previously I had internship experience with tech startups, including Accern and Space Jam Data, as well as having been a student consultant at NYU Data Services. Prior to that, I volunteered in the non-profit sector and had a technical paper accepted and presented at Bloomberg's Data for Good Exchange 2016, a nationwide civic data scinece conference.

Enjoying data journalism articles, I'm trying to gain skills in interactive data visualization. In the near future, I hope to keep developing data science products to address the specific needs across various organizations.
